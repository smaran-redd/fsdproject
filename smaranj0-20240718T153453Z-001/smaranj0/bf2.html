About.

Malla Reddy College of Engineering and Technology (MRCET) is a leading engineering college located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Affiliated with JNTUH and approved by AICTE, MRCET offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology, along with an MBA program. The college features state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a strong focus on research and innovation. MRCET maintains robust industry collaborations and has an active placement cell to ensure excellent career opportunities for students. The vibrant campus life includes various clubs, societies, and extracurricular activities, fostering overall personality development.
