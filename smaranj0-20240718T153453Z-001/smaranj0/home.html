Malla Reddy College of Engineering and Technology (MRCET) is a premier engineering institution located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It was established with the objective of providing high-quality technical education and fostering innovation and research in engineering and technology. The college is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH) and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Key Features and Offerings:

Academic Programs: MRCET offers a range of undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech) programs in various engineering disciplines such as Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, and Information Technology. The college also offers a Master of Business Administration (MBA) program. Faculty: The college boasts a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are dedicated to imparting quality education and mentoring students in their academic and professional pursuits. Infrastructure: MRCET is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, including modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a comprehensive library, and advanced computing facilities. The campus also provides amenities like hostels, sports facilities, and a cafeteria. Research and Innovation: The institution emphasizes research and development activities. It encourages students and faculty to engage in innovative projects and provides the necessary support and resources to foster a research-oriented environment. Industry Collaboration: MRCET maintains strong ties with the industry to facilitate internships, industrial visits, and collaborative projects. This helps students gain practical exposure and enhances their employability. Placements: The college has a dedicated placement cell that works tirelessly to ensure students secure job opportunities in reputed companies. The cell organizes various training programs, workshops, and campus recruitment drives to prepare students for the professional world. Student Life: MRCET offers a vibrant campus life with various clubs, societies, and extracurricular activities. Students can participate in technical fests, cultural events, sports, and community service initiatives, contributing to their overall personality development. Accreditation and Rankings: The college is known for its academic excellence and has received various accreditations and rankings from national and international bodies.
